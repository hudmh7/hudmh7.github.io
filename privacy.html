Coral Media Engine — Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 7, 2026

Coral Media Engine ("the App") is a personal content-publishing tool operated by Hudson Hagmann. It creates original short-form videos and publishes them to the operator's own TikTok account.

Information We Collect

The App accesses only the TikTok account information of its operator, via TikTok's official APIs after explicit OAuth authorization: basic profile information (display name, avatar) and video-publishing capability (upload/publish tokens). The App does not collect information about any other TikTok users, and has no end users other than its operator.

How Information Is Used

Access tokens are used solely to schedule and publish the operator's own original videos and to read back performance metrics of those videos. No data is sold, shared, or transferred to any third party.

Storage & Security

Tokens and data are stored on a private, self-hosted server controlled by the operator, protected by authentication and encrypted transport (HTTPS). Data is retained only while the App is in use and can be deleted at any time by disconnecting the TikTok account.

Your Choices

Authorization can be revoked at any time in TikTok's app settings (Settings > Security > Apps and websites), which immediately invalidates the App's access.
