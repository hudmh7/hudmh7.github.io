Coral Media Engine

Coral Media Engine is a private, single-operator publishing tool. It generates original educational short-form videos — AI-narrated rankings over licensed imagery — and schedules them for publishing to the operator's own social accounts via official platform APIs (including TikTok's Content Posting API).

The tool has no public sign-up and no third-party users.
